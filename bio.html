    • About the Artist

    Born Batu Pahat, Malaysia. Lives and works in New York.

    Statement

    Growing up in a Malaysian kampong (village), the youngest in a large, extended multi-generational family, I was exposed to the endless cycle of life and death, and beheld the power of all things living to adapt, transform, and evolve.

    Of all our abilities, I sense deeply that we being both rational[1] and emotional[2] beings who lie at the intersection of perception[3] and intuition[4], objectivity and subjectivity, is what makes us unique both as a species as well as individuals. How we balance such opposing forces in our hearts and minds, as well as within our communities will, I believe, determine our destiny.

    I hope to provide a lens into these powerful, but hidden, forces so that each of us in our own individual ways may search and find a path to harmony by resolving these complementary opposites, and perhaps glimpse some reason and purpose in the seemingly whimsical, random ways of nature.

    • 1. Rational [adjective] governed by, or showing evidence of, clear and sensible thinking and judgment, based on reason rather than emotion or prejudice
    • 2. Emotional [adjective] inspired or governed by emotion, and not by reason or willpower
    • 3. Perception [noun] using the senses to acquire information about the surrounding environment or situation
    • 4. Intuition [noun] being aware of or knowing something without having to discover or perceive it
